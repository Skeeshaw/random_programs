Independent Director - Director not associated with the company. Can still be on the board (AKA Senior Associate). Assigned to partners that do not belong to the business.

Director - Primary member of the Board of Directors.

====================DIVISION & ROLE EXPLANATION====================

Logistics - People who create & develop the functionings of a business.

Manufacturing - Producers of goods of any type.

Real Estate - Business that sell land, houses, and other type of property.

Food and Beverage - Businesses responsible for the sale of consumable items.

Wholesale - Businesses responsible for the sale of merchandise to retailers
